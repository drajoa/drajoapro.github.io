DIPLOMA PROGRAMS

At the diploma level, YMCA College of Business Study Jinja aims to develop well-rounded individuals committed to learning and making a positive contribution.

Our students will not only be technologically sound, but also socially aware and capable of thinking in a cross disciplinary manner to become leading entrepreneurs, policy makers and engineers.

Applicants admitted to YMCA College of Business Study Jinja who subsequently require UBTEB attestation of their diploma must meet the conditions stipulated by the Higher Institution of Learning to get their diploma attested.

Details are available at the College

For O’Level applicants, the requirements may vary regionally and can be checked by visiting the College.

 

 

CERTIFICATE PROGRAMS

Students at YMCA College of Business Study Jinja form a diverse batch of talented individuals from diferent regional and social backgrounds.

Admissions are conducted on a strict merit-basis, and the university has made available generous funding opportunities to ensure that no potential student is held back from studying at YMCA College of Business Study Jinja due to financial barriers.

Once a part of YMCA College of Business Study Jinja, students are provided an environment which encourages them to think outside the box, bring out their best and aim for excellence.
