Academics

Students at YMCA College of Business Studies Jinja form a diverse batch of talented individuals from diverse regional and social backgrounds. Admissions are conducted on a strict merit-basis, and the College has made available generous funding opportunities to ensure that no potential student is held back from studying at YMCA College of Business Studies Jinja due to financial barriers.

Once a part of YMCA College of Business Studies Jinja, students are provided an environment which encourages them to think outside the box, bring out their best and aim for excellence.
